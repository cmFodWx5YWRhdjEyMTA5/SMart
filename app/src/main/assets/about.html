About - Sahakar Mart

Sahakar Mart is a multipurpose co-operative society incorporated in 2017 at Ahmednagar, Maharashtra State with objective to provide platform for sellers and buyers from rural to urban areas to avail quality products at best price and strengthen co-operative movement within the State.
